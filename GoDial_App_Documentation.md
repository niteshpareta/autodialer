# GoDial CRM & Auto Dialer - Comprehensive App Documentation

## Executive Summary
GoDial is a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and auto-dialer mobile application designed for sales teams and businesses to streamline their contact management, calling operations, and customer interaction processes. The app combines powerful CRM capabilities with automated calling features to enhance productivity and sales efficiency.

## App Overview

### Name
- **App Name**: GoDial (also referred to as CRM in some screens)
- **Platform**: iOS (based on App Store screenshots)
- **Category**: Business/CRM/Sales Tool

### Core Purpose
The app serves as an all-in-one platform for:
- Contact management and organization
- Automated dialing and call management
- Lead tracking and scoring
- Task and stage management
- Payment tracking and invoicing
- Business analytics and reporting
